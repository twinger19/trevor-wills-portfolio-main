Dress

Anchored by → Kintentional (The Artisan)

Polished, refined, and crafted for the modern office. Dress leather goods centre on investment-quality belts and structured wallets designed to pair with softorial suits, double-breasted blazers, and high-rise tailored trousers. Hardware is understated — antique metallics and refined branding buckles replace flashy logos. Responsible Classic leathers in deep neutrals project quiet authority.

Belts — Dress
Classic 28–32mm width scaled for high-rise tailored trousers. Responsible Classic leather with cracked leather effects and embossed textures. Refined branding hardware in antique gold or silver finishes. Minimal visible stitching — clean edges for softorial suiting.
Wallets — Dress
Sleek, structured bifold and card-holder silhouettes. Premium leather with RFID-blocking capability. Minimal hardware — colour depth and material quality carry the story. Deep jewel tones and neutrals with subtle tonal embossing.

Refined Casual

Energized by → Empowered Play (Retro Sport)

Relaxed but intentional — bridging office and weekend. Refined casual leather goods embrace braided construction, double-ring closures, and versatile widths that work with chinos, linen trousers, and mineral-washed denim. Colour moves from strict neutrals into warm amber, teal, and khaki gold. Wallets are practical, portable, and finished with natural materials. This is the vacation-dinner belt and the everyday carry wallet.

Belts — Refined Casual
Braided features in Responsible Classic leather and natural fibre alternatives (linen, hemp). Double-ring closures and mini buckles for adjustable fit. Classic colorways with experimental bold combinations. 30–35mm widths bridging dress and casual trouser silhouettes.
Wallets — Refined Casual
Practical multi-pocket designs in flexible, portable constructions. Natural materials with leather accents — earthtone palette with pops of colour through hardware or stitching. Casual braiding or woven details. Travel-friendly dimensions with modular pocket systems.

Casual & Workwear

Grounded by → Geo Logic (Earth & Tech)

Built for terrain and daily use. Casual & workwear leather goods channel utility-first design — burnished metal buckles with intentional patina, stamped and tooled leather with aged character, and durable construction that stands up to outdoor pursuits. Geo Logic's earth-inspired palette drives finishes from iron-oxide rust to beeswax-treated natural dyes. Belts are structured for heavy use; wallets are reinforced with functional compartments and weather-resistant closures.

Belts — Casual & Workwear
Constructed for heavy use with low-impact metal hardware — brass, copper, zinc alloy. GRS recycled materials for sustainable updates. Aged-character finishes: burnished, oxidized, iron-oxide patina. Stamped, tooled, and engraved details. 32–35mm widths. Functional D-ring and snap closures.
Wallets — Casual & Workwear
Structured, protective construction in durable leather with workwear patina. Multiple compartments for on-the-go organization. GRS recycled leather and low-impact PU options. Reinforced edge details and utilitarian snap closures. Beeswax-treated finishes that develop rich, natural patina over time.

Geo Logic — Materials
Natural origins define S/S 27 leather goods. Geo Logic replicates erosion and oxidation patterns found in nature — beeswax finishes and natural dyes create sunlight-exposed leather with rich patina. Burnished metal hardware in iron oxide tones develops a reddish-brown rust patina over time, while stamped, tooled, and engraved leather patches reference geological strata.
