Season Direction

Spring/Summer 2027 celebrates the duality of free-spirited artisan craft and polished coastal confidence. For women's accessories, this translates to textural earthy pieces, eclectic hardware, and fresh prep-inspired refinement.

Artisan Craftsmanship
Handcrafted aesthetics with a boho edge. Natural textures, eclectic metallic accents, and accessories that feel collected over time — tactile, grounded, and sun-drenched.

01 — Desert Bazar

Bohemian craft,
sun-drenched soul

Handcrafted aesthetics with a boho edge — woven belts, pebble leather goods, and eclectic metallic accents for the woman who gravitates toward pieces that feel crafted, textural, and lived-in.

Key Colors
Wet sand, eucalyptus & ginger biscuit
Warm, grounded neutrals in Wet Sand anchor the palette. Eucalyptus brings a muted green with a natural, organic feel. Ginger Biscuit adds soft, sun-baked orange-brown warmth. Earth-toned layering with sun-washed neutrals and warm coppery highlights through accessories.

Eclectic metals, hammered & antiqued
Eclectic metallic accents define this narrative — bronzed rings, hammered hardware, and antiqued details. Double-ring closures and artisan buckles with hand-sculpted stamps. Subtle texture mixing across suede, canvas, and woven trims.

Natural fibers, pebble leather & woven textures

02 — Play It Proper

Coastal prep,
playful polish

Fresh, crisp, modern-preppy style blending coastal ease with tailored structure. Sporty-prep staples meet polished accessories — where classic tradition is refreshed with color, charm, and seaside sophistication.

Key Colors
Crimson, classic beige & ice blue
Crimson delivers bold, classic red — strong hits for preppy energy. Classic Beige provides a clean and timeless neutral base. Ice Blue brings cool, coastal crispness. Strong color blocking pairs crimson and navy for impact, while powder blues nod to the seaside.

Hardware & Trims
Clean, refined & minimalistic
Hardware stays clean and refined — shiny silvers, crisp buckles, and minimalistic finishes. Contrast piping adds structure and visual interest. Rope-tie details and subtle nautical cues bring a coastal sensibility without overwhelming the polished aesthetic.

Polished cottons, poplin & performance blends
Polished cottons, poplin, and lightweight twill create the crisp foundation. Performance-inspired blends add modern functionality. Subtle stripes and color blocking amplify the playful attitude. Clean, structured materials that hold shape while maintaining a breezy, coastal ease.

Key Takeaways

Design with intention

Embrace the Handmade
Woven trims, hammered hardware, pebble leather textures — Desert Bazar puts craftsmanship and natural imperfection center stage. Accessories that feel collected over time, tactile, grounded, and sun-drenched with global wanderlust.
Polish with Personality
Crisp buckles, color-blocked webbing, and bold crimson pops. Play It Proper brings coastal charm and modern preppy confidence — clean lines refreshed with playful color and light seaside sophistication.
