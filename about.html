About Me

Creative Strategy Director with 15+ years building brands and leading design teams

Who I Am

I'm a Creative Strategy Director who bridges the gap between creative vision and business results. Over 15 years, I've led design teams for global heritage brands like Levi's, Calvin Klein, and Tommy Hilfiger while also building an in-house brand from scratch and scaling it to national retail.

What sets me apart is my ability to see the whole picture from market trends and brand positioning to strategic alignment across departments. I partner with VPs, directors, and senior leaders in merchandising, product development, and operations to drive company-wide goals. I don't just direct creative work; I build the systems, processes, and cross-functional relationships that enable great design to happen consistently.

My Approach

Great creative work requires three things: strategic clarity (knowing what we're solving for), collaborative culture (bringing the best out of teams), and operational excellence (executing flawlessly under real-world constraints). I focus on all three.
