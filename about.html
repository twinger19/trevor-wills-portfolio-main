About Me

Senior Design Director. Sixteen years directing creative across 11 licensed global brands — and a founding member of the RAA team behind Damen + Hastings.

Who I Am

A Senior Design Director who bridges creative vision and commercial results. Over 16 years I've led design teams across a portfolio of 11 licensed global brands — Levi's, Calvin Klein, Tommy Hilfiger, Columbia, Cole Haan, Tommy Bahama, Dickies, Kenneth Cole, Dockers, Red Wing, and Guess — and served as Creative Director on the RAA team that built Damen + Hastings, an in-house brand we grew from a pop-up to a national wholesale partnership with Kohl's.

My edge is seeing market, brand, team, calendar, and P&L at the same time. I partner early with merchandising, product development, and operations leadership so creative decisions land as commercial ones. I direct the work, and I build the systems and relationships that let great design happen when I'm not in the room.

My Approach

Great creative work requires three things: strategic clarity (knowing what we're solving for), collaborative culture (bringing the best out of teams), and operational excellence (executing under real-world constraints). I focus on all three.
