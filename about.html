About

Where craft meets the P&L.

Senior Design Director. Sixteen years directing creative across a portfolio of global brands — with an industrial-design background and a habit of leaving teams and systems better than I found them.

Trevor Wills · Chicago, IL

I'm a Senior Design Director who bridges creative vision and commercial results. Over 16 years I've led design teams across a portfolio of 11 licensed global brands — Levi's, Calvin Klein, Tommy Hilfiger, Columbia, Cole Haan, Tommy Bahama, Dickies, Kenneth Cole, Dockers, Red Wing, and Guess — plus private-label programs for Target, Walmart, Macy's, Costco, Kohl's, and JCPenney.

My edge is seeing market, brand, team, calendar, and P&L at the same time. I partner early with merchandising, product development, and operations leadership so creative decisions land as commercial ones. I direct the work — and I build the systems and relationships that let great design happen when I'm not in the room.

I came up through industrial design, so I'm just as comfortable talking construction, materials, and manufacturing as I am art-directing a campaign. Lately that's meant championing digital product creation and responsible materials across the org — bringing the team to where the industry is heading.

My approach

Great creative work needs three things: strategic clarity (knowing what we're solving for), a collaborative culture (bringing the best out of teams), and operational excellence (executing under real-world constraints). I focus on all three.
