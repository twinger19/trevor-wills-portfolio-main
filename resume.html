Outcome: 66% fewer physical samples, 35% faster speed-to-market, 2 weeks cut from the development cycle, and 75% of approvals moved to digital.

Sustainability across the product org

Emerging-tech translation

Founding member of Randa's AI Trailblazers group, rolling out generative AI tooling (Vizcom.AI, image generation, research workflows) into trend research, concept ideation, and design across the company.
